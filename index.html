Mindfulness

Meditation

Mindfulness is generally defined as non-judgmental, moment-by-moment awareness of our thoughts, feelings, bodily sensations, and surrounding environment. Exact definitions vary by this is usually accepted as such by practitioners such as Jon Kabat-Zinn. When we practice mindfulness, our thoughts stop rebashing the past or imagining the future and tune into what wa are sensing in the present moment. Mindfulness involves acceptance, and as such is non-judgmental. We pay attention to our thoughts and feelings without judging them. There is no right or wrong way to think or feel in a given moment.
